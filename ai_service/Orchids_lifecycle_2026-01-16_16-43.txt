AI Life Cycle & Care Advice
---------------------------------
Current Stage: Newly Planted / Establishment
Confidence: 0.9

Care:
Watering: Water once a week by soaking the bark and then draining all excess water. Never let the crown or roots sit in standing water.
Fertilizer: Apply a balanced orchid fertilizer at half-strength every two weeks during active growth.
Sunlight: Provide bright, filtered light. Avoid direct midday sun which can burn the leaves.
Pruning: Cut back the flower spike to the base or a node once the blooms have completely withered.
Soil: Replace loamy soil immediately with a coarse mix of fir bark, perlite, and charcoal to allow root respiration.
Pests: Monitor for mealybugs, scale, and spider mites on the undersides of leaves.

Red Flags:
- Black or mushy roots (root rot)
- Yellowing leaves
- Wrinkled or leathery leaves (dehydration)
- White cottony masses (mealybugs)
