Current Stage: Sowing/Germination

Care:
Watering: Keep soil constantly submerged under 2 inches of water or maintain extreme saturation.
Fertilizer: Apply nitrogen-rich fertilizer during the early vegetative stage and a balanced fertilizer during booting.
Sunlight: Requires at least 6-8 hours of direct light; if indoors, use high-intensity grow lights.
Pruning: Not required, but remove any dead or diseased leaves as they appear.
Soil: Heavy clay is ideal to prevent water drainage.
Pests: Watch for aphids or spider mites if kept indoors; use neem oil if necessary.

Red Flags:
- Yellowing leaves (potential nitrogen deficiency)
- Brown spots on leaves (Rice Blast fungus)
- Soil surface drying out
- Stunted growth due to insufficient light
